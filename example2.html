Example 2: Integration with Lightbox

An example of Slideshow integration with Lightbox (click on the show to open the current slide in a modal window). In this case I am using a stripped-down and customized version of Lightbox by Sam Birch, however any Mootools 1.2-compatible Lightbox (Slimbox etc) will do. Note the lightbox.css stylesheet is required for the proper functioning of the script. Also note the rel="lightbox" attribute in the HTML anchor, this is the hook Lightbox uses to determine which links to attach itself to. See the next example...
